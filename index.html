About the US comparison

US university spinout terms vary widely. Many deals take a straight 5% (or less) dilutable stake with no protection at all. Only some include an anti-dilution protection cap, and when they do, the cap is typically in the €2–15M range, with €20M at the extreme.

The "5% non-dilutable up to €10M" framing that sometimes appears in policy documents as the US standard is therefore one specific example structure, not necessarily the default. This tool lets you compare it against a dilutable stake to see how the two actually behave across exit sizes.
